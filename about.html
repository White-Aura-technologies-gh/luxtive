Our Policies

At Luxtive Jewelry, we believe in transparent and straightforward pricing. The price listed for each product on our website is the exact and final price for the item itself. This price reflects the quality of the materials, the intricacy of the craftsmanship, and the exclusivity of the design.

Please Note: The displayed price does not include shipping or delivery fees. These charges are calculated separately based on your delivery location and will be paid independently from the item's cost. This allows us to provide fair and accurate shipping rates for all our customers, both local and international.

Your privacy is of utmost importance to us. All personal information collected is used solely for the purpose of processing your order and enhancing your customer experience. We do not share your data with third parties.

Intellectual Property & Image Rights: All images, photographs, and content displayed on this website are the exclusive property of Luxtive Jewelry. They are protected by copyright law and are intended for the sole purpose of showcasing our products to our customers. Unauthorized use, reproduction, or distribution of our images for commercial purposes, including but not limited to use on other websites, social media for sales, or any form of advertising, is strictly prohibited.

Discovery of such unauthorized use will result in immediate legal action to protect our intellectual property. We invest significantly in our product photography and branding, and we will enforce our rights to the fullest extent of the law.

We want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If you wish to make an exchange, please adhere to the following guidelines:

  • For customers within Ghana: Exchanges must be initiated within 3 days (72 hours) of receiving your order. The item must be in its original, unworn condition and in its original packaging.
  • For international customers: Please contact our support team within 72 hours of delivery to discuss exchange options.
  • All exchanges are subject to a quality assurance inspection. Items that are damaged, altered, or show signs of wear may not be eligible for an exchange.

We are pleased to offer both nationwide and worldwide delivery to bring our exquisite pieces to you, wherever you are.

  • Accra & Environs: Delivery is typically completed within 24-48 hours.
  • Outside Accra (Within Ghana): Deliveries to other regions in Ghana generally take 48-72 hours.
  • International Shipping: For our global customers, delivery times range from 4 to 15 business days, depending on the destination country and its customs procedures.

Please note that delivery fees are separate from the product price and are payable upon receipt of your order.
